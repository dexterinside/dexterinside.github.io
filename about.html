About Us

Official Hacker Space @ IIIT Delhi

Dexterinside is one of its kind student run makers space in New-Delhi,India. Its goal is to provide fusion of technology, learning and collaboration. You can build anything you want over here . The best part of our space is we do not charge any fees from students, we believe things are best utilized when it is free.

We give you the freedom to explore the space and the tools without any questions asked, it is open to anyone with a passion within to build something new on his own. We also provide guidance towards your projects within our space.

We are a collaborative makerspace established on April 18th 2014. We are focussed on providing free access to electronics instruments at any time of the day. Our focus is to motivate out of the box ideas and transform those ideas into reality.

Apart from the space we also conduct workshops/tutorials on various engineering topics. Which you can refer to in our “Upcoming Events” page. We also encourage you to share your knowledge with the community by conducting workshops in our space. Please contact us if you are interested in conducting one.
